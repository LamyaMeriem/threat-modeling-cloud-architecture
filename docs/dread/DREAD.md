The DREAD methodology was applied to the most critical threats identified during the STRIDE analysis.

| Threat | D | R | E | A | D | Score | Level |
|------|---|---|---|---|---|-------|-------|
| Spoofing HR Portal | 5 | 4 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 4.8 | High |
| SQL Injection | 5 | 4 | 4 | 5 | 4 | 4.4 | High |
| DoS HR Portal | 5 | 5 | 4 | 2 | 4 | 4.0 | High |
